Study Skills

Study skills are not only important, they are transferrable into any task.
In order to manage my time, I have been utilising the "pomodoro technique".
This has helped me break down the work into small fragments.
There are 4 steps to the pomodoro technique:

  • - Set a task
  • - Break down work into 25 minute chunks
  • - Take a 5 minute break - get up and leave the workspace
  • - Once you have gone through 4 cycles take a 20 minute break

To help me retain information for exams I have been writing my notes on paper.
This is a simple yet effective technique that not only helps to commit my notes to long term memory;
it also enables me to write my notes more efficiently and in way that will resonate with me more when I try to recall my notes.
